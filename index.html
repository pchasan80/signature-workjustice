How to use in Outlook (Desktop):

  1. Click the "Copy Signature" button above.
  2. Open Outlook and click New Email.
  3. Click on Signature > Signatures... in the toolbar.
  4. Click New to create a signature.
  5. Paste (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V) the signature into the Edit signature box.
  6. Click OK to save.

How to use in Gmail:

  1. Click the "Copy Signature" button above.
  2. Open Gmail and go to Settings > See all settings.
  3. Scroll down to the Signature section.
  4. Create a new signature or edit an existing one.
  5. Paste (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V) the signature into the text box.
  6. Scroll to the bottom and click Save Changes.
